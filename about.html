About this diary

DataDiary is a running log of things I learn, break, and figure out while working with Adobe's data stack — primarily Customer Journey Analytics and the Experience Platform.

Most posts start as personal notes. The kind of thing I wish existed when I was searching for answers at 11pm before a client call. If they're useful to someone else, even better.

I work with data for a living — schema design, data flows, reporting layers, the whole pipeline. This is where I write about the parts that don't make it into official docs.
